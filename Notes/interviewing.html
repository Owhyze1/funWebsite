Interviewing Notes

Technical Interviews

After 20 minutes on a problem, look at the solution to understand it.

For Leetcode practice:

Focus on medium to hard problems will get you 80% of the way in terms of doing interviews

Binary Trees are more advanced and want to nail the easier problems first before doing these.


Preparing for Job Search

Set aside time to increase network, to work on projects, to practice leetcode

What Are Three Things That You Want This Person to Know About You

Craft your story and/or responses to their questions around those 3 things. If you are very clear about what your top 3 points are, then it will be very easy for them to say that this person has these 3 qualities. Then everyone else that you spoke with will be on the same page about these same 3 qualities.

Tools for Outreach

  • Leverage LinkedIn to find companies/people to reach out to
  • skreak - plugin that tells if people opened your email and it lets you know if you should follow up with them. If you follow up at least 2 or 3 times, you increase the response rate by 20 - 30%
  • Career Guides - Came up with own email template, where he customized the first line of the email
